Instructions and information

Feed them half a pack of Hydra Care each

These are stored at eye-height, in the right-hand side of the closet by the front door. See box in right of the photo below

  • Shake well first!!!!!
  • Feed Stu and Beans half a pack each. Use the small plates in the cupboard above the sink
  • Beans usually won't finish his half in one sitting, so try to make sure Stu doesn't drink all of Beans'. If there are any leftovers by the time you leave, you can leave the plates out by their feeder
