Water Jug

Instructions and information

We got this as a 'back up' water source in case the fountain malfunctions. Please keep it in the sink, because Beans will play with it and paw water out.

  • Please refill the jug with fresh water. To open: Twist the jug (white part) off the black base, then unscrew the black "lid".
  • Refill the jug till it's full. Make sure the black plug (circled below) is pushed in all the way and totally tight.
  • Wash the metal bowl. Replace in base and fill the bowl with water till itâ€™s almost full.
  • Screw jug back on, it might gurgle a bit to refill the bowl again.
